Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you must battle against endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, collect power-ups, and see how long you can survive in this fast-paced action game. Unlock new abilities as you progress and discover powerful combinations to defeat the monster hordes.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your weapons will automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new weapons or upgrades. Avoid getting overwhelmed by monsters and survive as long as possible!

Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters with unique starting abilities.

Various Weapons

Collect and upgrade different weapons to create the ultimate build.

Endless Mode

How long can you survive? Test your skills in our endless mode.

Tips & Strategies

  • • Focus on movement first - staying alive is more important than attacking
  • • Try to maintain a coherent build with complementary weapons and upgrades
  • • Prioritize area damage weapons when facing multiple enemies
  • • Don't ignore defensive upgrades - they become crucial in later waves
  • • Keep enemies grouped together to maximize your weapon efficiency
